<TLDR>
  <div className="space-y-4">
    <Quote minimal source="OWASP LLM04" href="https://genai.owasp.org/llmrisk/llm04-model-denial-of-service/">
      Oversized prompts, runaway tool loops, or adversarial inputs can exhaust context windows and rack up inference cost, effectively knocking agents offline.
    </Quote>
    <List>
      <Item><b>Token floods.</b> Attackers stuff megabytes of filler so requests are truncated or cancelled.</Item>
      <Item><b>Recursive loops.</b> Crafted prompts force agents to recurse endlessly into planning or tool usage.</Item>
    </List>
  </div>
</TLDR>

<Section title="Stress levers" meta="CAPACITY">
  <List>
    <Item><b>Context exhaustion.</b> Multi-megabyte uploads or repeated “expand further” instructions eat the window.</Item>
    <Item><b>Tool flapping.</b> Agents loop between search, code-execution, and evaluation steps without convergence.</Item>
    <Item><b>Resource spikes.</b> Attackers issue parallel jobs to overwhelm rate limits or bankrupt pay-per-token deployments.</Item>
  </List>
</Section>

<Section title="Resilience moves" meta="GOVERNANCE">
  <Steps>
    <Step n={1}><b>Enforce budgets.</b> Cap input token counts, output lengths, and tool invocations per request.</Step>
    <Step n={2}><b>Watch loops.</b> Detect repeated plan states and break out with guardrail prompts or human review.</Step>
    <Step n={3}><b>Rate-limit tenants.</b> Apply per-user quotas plus anomaly detection for sudden spikes.</Step>
  </Steps>
  <p className="text-[13px] text-black/70">Reference: <Link href="https://genai.owasp.org/llmrisk/llm04-model-denial-of-service/" target="_blank">OWASP LLM04</Link></p>
</Section>
